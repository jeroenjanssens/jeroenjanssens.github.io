About

Jeroen is a Senior Data Scientist at YPlan in New York City. He has an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence and a Ph.D. in Machine Learning. He is authoring a book titled Data Science at the Command Line, which will be published by O'Reilly in October 2014. Jeroen enjoys biking the Brooklyn Bridge, building tools, and eating stroopwafels.
